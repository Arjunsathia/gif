A premium, interactive "Will you be my Valentine?" surprise featuring adorable cat memes, glassmorphism design, and playful interactions.

## Features
- **Premium Aesthetics**: Modern glassmorphism design with soft gradients, blur effects, and elegant typography (`Outfit` and `Dancing Script`).
- **Interactive Cat Memes**: Cute kitten images that change based on your clicks.
- **Dynamic Button Logic**:
  - The **YES** button grows significantly as you click NO.
  - The **NO** button shrinks, fades, and eventually disappears once the YES button is large enough.
- **Heart Particle System**: Multiple types of hearts and sparkles burst from clicks.
- **Celebration Overlay**: A smooth, modal-style celebration with a cute cat GIF.
- **Mobile & Large Screen Optimized**: Mobile-first design that scales beautifully to large monitors.

## How to Run
Open `index.html` in your browser.

## Customization
- **Messages**: Edit `js/script.js` to change the `messages` array.
- **Styles**: Modify `css/style.css` to update colors or glassmorphism settings.
- **Memes**: Update the URLs in `js/script.js` to use different GIF links.
